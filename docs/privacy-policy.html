InHausKI – Privacy Policy

100% Offline  ·  No data collection

Effective date: April 12, 2026
App: InHausKI – Offline AI Chat
Developer: InHausKI

1. Overview

InHausKI is an offline artificial-intelligence chat application. All AI processing, document storage, and chat history are handled entirely on your device. No personal data, no chat messages, and no documents ever leave your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not transmit chat messages, documents, or queries to any server.
  • We do not use analytics, crash-reporting SDKs, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

3. Network Access

InHausKI requires a one-time internet connection only during initial setup to download the AI model file (~2.9 GB) and optionally the embedding model (~90 MB) from Hugging Face (huggingface.co). After setup, the app operates fully offline. The download URL is the only external endpoint the app ever contacts. No user data is included in this request.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • AI model files – the downloaded GGUF model files stored in the app's private storage directory.
  • Chat history – your conversation messages, stored in a local SQLite database inside the app's private storage. This data is never synced or backed up to the cloud.
  • Document index – text chunks from documents you import, stored in a local vector database (ObjectBox) inside the app's private storage.
  • App settings – preferences such as language and GPU mode, stored via Android SharedPreferences.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device's Settings, or by using the "Reset setup" option within the app.

5. Permissions

InHausKI requests only the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET – used solely for the one-time model download described in Section 3.

No storage, camera, microphone, contacts, or location permissions are requested or used.

6. Children's Privacy

InHausKI does not target children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). Since no personal data is collected, COPPA/GDPR-K compliance is inherent. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact us.

7. GDPR / Data Subject Rights (EU Users)

Because InHausKI does not collect or process any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data held by the developer to which GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) would apply. All data resides exclusively on your own device and is under your full control.

8. Changes to This Policy

If a future version of InHausKI introduces data collection, this policy will be updated before that version is published. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository or contact the developer directly via the support link on the Google Play store listing.
